## The reality

Balancing graduate coursework with finance recruiting requires strict calendar ownership.

## Habits I rely on

- Time blocks for deep technical study.
- Weekly priorities limited to three high-impact outcomes.
- Daily shutdown routine to capture pending actions.

## Result

I stay consistent under pressure and avoid losing momentum during heavy recruiting windows.
