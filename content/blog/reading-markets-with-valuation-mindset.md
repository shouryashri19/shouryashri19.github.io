## A practical lens

Market commentary is most useful when translated into valuation drivers. I focus on three questions:

- What changed in growth expectations?
- What changed in risk and discount rates?
- What changed in margin durability?

## Applying the lens

When a sector rerates, I map the move to assumptions in my model. If assumptions cannot justify pricing, I flag it for deeper analysis.

## Why this matters

This process helps me avoid reactive conclusions. It forces explicit reasoning and supports better long-term judgment.
